Steve Jobs

ABOUT

  • February 24, 1955 - Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco to Joanne Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. The then-unmarried couple give up their son to adoption. Paul and Clara Jobs become Jobs' non-biological parents.
  • 1970 : Meets future Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak through a friend.
  • 1976 : Co-founds Apple Computer with Wozniak and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1980 : Apple goes public, putting Jobs' net worth north of $200 million.
  • 1985 : CEO John Sculley engineers Jobs' ouster from Apple. Jobs resigns as Apple chairman and starts NeXT Computer, funded by selling $70 million of his Apple stock.
  • 1996 : Returns to Apple, as an adviser, after it buys NeXT for $429 million.
  • 2001 : Introduces the iPod.
  • 2004 : Jobs undergoes surgery to remove a cancerous tumor in his pancreas.
  • 2011 : Goes on medical leave of absence from CEO post nearly two years after the six-month break he took to undergo a liver transplant.
  • 2011 : Gives a keynote speech at Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference, it's Jobs' last public appearance at an Apple event.
